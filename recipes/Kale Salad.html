  • Oranges
  • Tablespoon orange juice
  • Sherry Vinegar
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 pound kale
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  1. Peel the oranges with a sharp knife.
  2. Squeeze out the needed juice and reserve.
  3. Remove the tough stems from the kale and discard.
  4. Toss together with the cranberries and vinaigrette.
  5. Serve on a platter scattered with orange segments and sprinkled with the almonds.
